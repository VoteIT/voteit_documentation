.. _glossary:

Glossary
========

.. glossary::
  :sorted:
   
  Access policy
    
    Handles access to a meeting. Example of common ones are public or closed access policy.
    The moderator may change access policy via **Settings** > **Access policy**

  Agenda

    In VoteIT the menu on your left hand side when you're within a meeting. It has the heading Agenda.
    Below it you'll find links to all agenda items.
